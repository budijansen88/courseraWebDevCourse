Odd Taxi Mini Review

Odd Taxi is an anime that aired this year (2021). It's really good in my opinion, the story is really interesting, out-of-place scenes makes the show really funny too.

like there's this scene where okodawa (the main protagonist which is the seal) and the alpaca women is talking about some serious things in the park in the middle of the night.

and the alpaca women just suddenly started dancing? after that she immediately kicks the air showing odokawa her martial art self defense kick i think. it was such an unexpectable thing to happen but it really made the show hilarious.
